Clinic with Pro Beach Player:

Ryan Mariano

 

 

HE'S BACK!!! 

 

Ryan Mariano returns to Baltimore Beach for another in his series of sand volleyball clinics with a pro.  Ryan loves to share his passion and knowledge of the sport through clinics and lessons. During the 2011 season, Ryan will be running clinics throughout the United States, including here in Baltimore, MD.
 

Ryan, born and raised in Lake Forest, California has competed and excelled on volleyball courts indoors and out for the past twenty-two years. Ryan played for the Long Beach State Forty-Niners where he led the NCAA in aces and digs per game in his sophomore, junior and senior campaigns. Ryan went on to play international professional indoor volleyball in Austria and Switzerland.

 

"Ryan brings a lot of energy and knowledge to The Sand Club clinics.  Ryan's interaction with each and every adult and junior is the biggest benefit of having Ryan running clinics." -Allen Alexander
